Machines and Meows

There once was a robot...

Who stood all alone

Performing her duties from dusk until dawn

Her machinery whirred
and her circutry hummed

As she sorted the scraps
in the junkyard she worked

But she stopped in her tracks

When she heard a loud

CRASH

Her head spun
and her eyes scanned for the source

Only to find a small orange cat

The robot slowly approached

And her new furry freind

Meowed and purred

As her hard metal hand
pet his soft fuzzy head

And from that day on...

They were best of freinds
